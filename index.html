Introduction

This paper examines how artificial intelligence changes quality management in accounting firms and what these changes mean for future professional practice.

The introduction frames AI as both a technological and organizational force. It outlines why accounting firms are under pressure to modernize quality management procedures while maintaining professional judgment, compliance, and trust.

The website structure follows the paper's logic: first the theoretical background establishes the conceptual vocabulary, then the methodology explains the research design, the results present the findings, and the discussion evaluates their meaning before the final remarks close the argument.
