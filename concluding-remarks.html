Summary and Conclusion

The conclusion returns to the research question and answers it directly. It should synthesize the argument rather than introduce a new line of analysis.

A clear conclusion shows how the theoretical background, methodology, results, and discussion fit together into one coherent contribution.

The paper closes by making its central argument visible one final time: what was asked, what was found, and why it matters.
